(1) What will you do? (1-2 paragraphs)
    I plan to write a GameBoy emulator which supports playing games with input
    and graphical output. I will accomplish this using a graphics/input toolkit
    (SDL, GLFW, raw OpenGL, etc) and by implementing the GameBoy ISA in C.

    I may support something like audio or one of the GameBoy accessories (see
    the GameBoy printer, camera, 4-player adapter, or Game Genie/Shark
    cards). However this is subject to timing.

(2) Who are the team members?
    Me.

(3) What research did you do? Links to articles, papers, etc.
    I skimmed some documentation to see how complicated the architecture is:
    https://gbdev.io/pandocs/, https://github.com/gbdev/pandocs
    https://gekkio.fi/files/gb-docs/gbctr.pdf

    I have looked at some testing ROMs to test an emulator throughout
    development without needing enough to support a full game.
    https://github.com/retrio/gb-test-roms

    I have skimmed the SDL3 and GLFW documentation to determine what GUI and
    input tools I would want to use. Either one seems fine.
    https://wiki.libsdl.org/SDL3/FrontPage
    https://www.glfw.org/documentation.html
